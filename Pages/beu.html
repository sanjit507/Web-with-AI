Mission of Bihar Engineering University, Patna

  • To develop flexible and innovative academic programmes to cater for the needs of students with different backgrounds, and prepare them to compete at the national and international levels.
  • To emphasise knowledge creation through a well-planned research programme in emerging areas, and application of knowledge by undertaking issues and problems from the industry.
  • To emphasise the overall development of a well-rounded personality of its students with both professional and emotional maturity to take up the challenges in their chosen profession, and to contribute to the development of society.
