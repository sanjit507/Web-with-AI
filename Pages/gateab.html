What is GATE Exam - Full Information

Introduction

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is an entrance exam that provides opportunities for higher education (M.Tech), high-paying jobs, and PSU jobs. With strong preparation, you can also crack government exams like ISRO, DRDO, NTPC, and BARC.

Who Conducts the GATE Exam?

GATE is conducted by IISc Bangalore and the 7 old IITs:

  • IIT Delhi
  • IIT Roorkee
  • IIT Kanpur
  • IIT Bombay
  • IIT Madras
  • IIT Kharagpur
  • IIT Guwahati

Minimum Qualification/Eligibility

  • 3rd Year B.Tech/BE Students
  • M.Sc, BPharma, BArch, BSc, MCA/MA, MTech

Why GATE?

  • High-paying PSU jobs
  • Higher education in IITs, IIMs, and foreign universities
  • Central and state government careers
  • Campus recruitment benefits

Details About PSUs

PSUs (Public Sector Undertakings) are government-owned companies categorized into CPSEs, SLPEs, and Autonomous Bodies.

GATE Exam Pattern

Particulars Details
Examination Mode Computer-Based Test (CBT)
Total Time 3 Hours
Number of Papers 29
Type of Questions MCQs, MSQs, NAT
Total Questions 65
Negative Marking Only for MCQs; no negative marking for MSQs & NAT
