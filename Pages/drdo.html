Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) at DIBER, Haldwani

The Defence Institute of Bio-Energy Research (DIBER), Haldwani, under the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), is inviting applications for the position of Junior Research Fellow (JRF). This opportunity is ideal for candidates passionate about research in bio-energy and related fields.

Vacancy Details

The institute is offering a total of 3 JRF positions across various disciplines:

  • Engineering Disciplines: Computer Science, Electronics Engineering, Electronics & Instrumentation, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, and similar subjects.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the JRF position, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • Educational Qualification: A first-class Graduate degree in the relevant engineering discipline or a Postgraduate degree in Chemistry with first division at both Graduate and Post Graduate levels. Candidates should also have a valid NET/GATE qualification from a recognized institute or university.
  • Age Limit: The upper age limit is 28 years as of the date of the interview. Age relaxation is applicable as per government rules (3 years for OBC and 5 years for SC/ST candidates).

Stipend

Selected candidates will receive a monthly stipend of ₹37,000 along with House Rent Allowance (HRA) as per DRDO norms.

Selection Process

The selection process involves a walk-in interview. Shortlisted candidates will be notified about the interview schedule and venue. It is advisable to check the official DRDO DIBER website or contact the institute directly for the most current information.

How to Apply

Interested candidates should submit their applications via email to the addresses provided in the official notification. Ensure that all necessary documents, including self-attested copies of degrees and certificates, are attached with the application.

Important Dates

  • Last Date for Application Submission: January 15, 2025
  • Interview Date: January 17, 2025
